Unlock eligibility for phones, tablets, and mobile Internet devices on Postpaid plans like T-Mobile ONE
The device must have been active on the T-Mobile network for at least 40 days on the requesting line.

Unlock eligibility for phones, tablets, and mobile Internet devices on Prepaid plans like Pay in Advance
